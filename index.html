🖍️ Sample Pages

🐯 Isabella Tiger Moth

This fluffy moth starts life as the fuzzy Woolly Bear caterpillar! Some say it can predict the weather. It hibernates in ice and survives freezing temperatures!

🪲 Cactus Longhorn Beetle

This shiny black beetle is often found munching on prickly pear pads. Its long antennae help it explore the desert floor.

🌵 Prickly Pear Cactus

This cactus has edible fruits called tunas and flowers that attract bees, birds, and beetles. Even its spines offer habitat for desert critters!

🐝 Xylocopa Bee

This large carpenter bee drills nests in wood and pollinates wildflowers and desert plants. Buzzing with strength and purpose!
